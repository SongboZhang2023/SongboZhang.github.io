About me

I am a researcher at ICQD, the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC). My research field is theoretical condensed matter physics, focusing on novel quantum states of matter (e.g., topological insulators/semimetals, quantum Hall effects, and unconventional superconductivity), their unique quantum transport properties and potential functionality/applications. I also work on non-Hermitian (topological) physics.

Research Interests

Quantum transport in single/hybrid quantum materials

Topological states of matter

Majorana-based quantum computation

Non-Hermitian topological phenomena
